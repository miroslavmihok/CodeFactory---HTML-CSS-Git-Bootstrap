Interstellar (2014)

Directed by: Christopher Nolan

Cast: Matthew McConaughey, Anne Hathaway, Matt Damon

About:

Interstellar is a 2014 epic science fiction film co-written,
directed, and produced by Christopher Nolan. Set in a dystopian
future where humanity is embroiled in a catastrophic
blight and famine, the film follows a group of astronauts
who travel through a wormhole near Saturn in
search of a new home for humankind.
